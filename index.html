About Chroma11y

Chroma11y helps designers and developers create beautiful, accessible color combinations that comply with WCAG accessibility standards.

How it works

Enter your text and background colors, and Chroma11y will calculate the contrast ratio and check it against WCAG 2.1 standards:

  • AA Large Text (3:1) - For large text (18pt+) and UI components
  • AA Small Text (4.5:1) - For regular text under 18pt
  • AAA Large Text (4.5:1) - Enhanced contrast for large text
  • AAA Small Text (7:1) - Maximum contrast for small text

If your colors don't meet the standards, we'll suggest alternatives that do.
